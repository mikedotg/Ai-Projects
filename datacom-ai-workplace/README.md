# Datacom | Partnering with AI in the Workplace

## Intro & Scenario
Meet your team members and learn about your project goals. You'll collaborate with a small, dynamic team across messaging tools and in-person chats.

### Your Role (Mike - Graduate Associate)
Partner with AI tools to enhance productivity, improve outcomes, and support your team in practical ways. You’re not expected to know how to code — your job is to think clearly, prompt effectively, and communicate well with both humans and AI.

### Your Goal
Complete a series of tasks that demonstrate how AI can support workplace problem-solving — including writing prompts, designing presentations, and debugging code — all without needing a tech background.

### Your Team
- **Maya (Manager / Product Lead):** Direct and supportive. Maya values initiative and clear communication. She loves seeing junior team members try things out and reflect on their process.
- **Jordan (Coworker / Fellow Graduate):** Always experimenting with new AI tools. Jordan’s curious, enthusiastic, and a bit competitive — they’ll often ping you to compare notes.
- **Sam (Coworker / Senior Developer):** Technical, helpful, but short on time. Sam appreciates when you’ve thought through a problem before asking for help — especially if you’ve tried AI first.
