Recommended Next Steps: A Low-Risk Path to Automation

  1. Phase 1 Read-Only Proof of Concept (2 weeks): Deploy OrderBot in observation mode. It reads and processes orders but takes no action — a human verifies its decisions. This validates accuracy with zero risk.
  2. Phase 2 Supervised Automation (4 weeks): OrderBot processes orders autonomously but flags exceptions for human approval. Staff review a daily summary dashboard.
  3. Phase 3 Full Autonomy: Once accuracy targets are consistently met, OrderBot operates independently with exception-only human intervention.
